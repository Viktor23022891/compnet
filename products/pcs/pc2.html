Obsidian Edge

Mid-Tier Performance Build

The Obsidian Edge is built for gamers who demand more than entry-level can offer. With excellent performance at 1440p and superior multitasking ability, it’s a strong all-rounder for gaming, productivity, and light streaming.

  • Strong cooling with great airflow
